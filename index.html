2022 Season for Ferrari

Constructors Championship
1. Red Bull Racing 6. Alfa Romeo

3. Mercedes 8. Haas
4. Alpine Renault 9. Alphatauri
5. Mclaren 10. Williams

After a very promising start to the season with a 1-2 in F1's first race of the year in Bahrain, the Scuderia drivers would fail to continue that form for the remainder of the season. With some key mistakes early on in the upcoming races from mainly Carlos Sainz, alongside some poor management race-time decisions from the staff, they could never reach that sweet 1-2 finish again during the season. Carlos would however find his form midway through the season with his first career pole-position and first career race win that gave Ferrari a hope for a title they haven't acheived since 2008. Charles was steady throughout the season, picking up 3 wins out of the 22 races in this years calender and placing top-6 in 19 of them. Unfortunately for the Scuderia team, the title would soon be out of reach mainly thanks to Red Bull's Max Verstappens record breaking year. With that being said they managed to squeeze in a 2nd place, their best performance since 2019. So things are looking up, and with both drivers returning for 2023, a very exciting future may be in front of the prancing horses.
